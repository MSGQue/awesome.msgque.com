Clear space and minimum size

The reference for the minimum clear space is defined by x, measured by the width of the lower part of the stork. A minimum clear space of 2x should always be applied. Try to maximize clear space whenever possible.

The minimum size of our logo and is 32px on screen, or 0.5in (12.7mm) in print, measured by the height of the height.

Minimum size: 32px on screen | 0.5in (12.7mm) in print
